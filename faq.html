Frequently Asked Questions About JMU

We know entering a new chapter of your life can be scary, so we're here to answer any questions you may have!

Commonly Asked Questions:

What is the tuition at JMU?
Please visit our tuition page for an in depth look at JMU tuition and what your money goes to.
How can I apply to JMU?
This page contains all the information an aspiring JMU Duke needs in order to become a part of our community.
Does JMU have a mens swim team?
No, in order to maintain our Title IX standards, as well as a lack of interest, we do not have a mens swim team. JMU does however have a well established club swim team!
What's your favorite aspect of being a JMU Duke!?
My favorite aspect of being a student at JMU is the inclusion and overall kindness seen on campus! During my time at JMU I have continued to meet helpful and knowledgeable staff, as well as kind and friendly students. Everyone is a friend here at JMU!
